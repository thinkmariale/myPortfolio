  • (Nov. 2014)CMU

    LinguAR:

    LinguAR is a language learning application on Google Glass.

    We are taking advantage of this wearable device by collecting as much data as possible from the user's daily life. The application aggregates what the users has been saying throughout the day to create what we call: "smart categories". This categories consist of words related to what the user has been talking about. We use them to build a tailored Lesson Plan for the user, which will teach the user about content we know he is interested on because he has been talking about it throughout the day.

    I was one of the programmers in the project; and I was responsible for the data collection and the smart categories. Once th data was aggregated for the user, it was send to the Lesson Plan which I also helped implement.

  • (Apr. 2014)CMU

    Laura - Virtual Peer:

    A virtual peer for financial literacy learning. Focused on learning through teaching by helping Laura solve her problems. Its targeted to students age 18-25.

    The virtual peer is the main source of motivation to make users want to come back and use Laura again. Through several playtest we have discovered that user want to use her again because after playing, they realize how much more they need to learn and how important it is to know about financial literacy (which was the main goal of the project) (Carnegie Mellon University).

  • (Apr. 2008)RPI

    Fusion Sky Games:

    FusionSkyGames was a result of a research I did in human computer interaction (HCI) to increase learning in children in developing countries and economically challenged areas.

    After five months of HCI research and field study in Bogotá, Colombia and Troy, NY; I applied my learning's and conclusions in a game called NumberMission. During the development of the game I continued doing field study with the children of Bogotá, Colombia and Troy to guarantee that my game would impact kids and increase learning in both math and recycling (the main focus of the game).(WebPage).
