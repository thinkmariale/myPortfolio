About Bot

“A color for the weather” @space_nietzsche

Space Nietzsche runs on nodeJS. Every 20 min. Space Nietzsche selects a city and grabs its current weather information. Once I have this information it uses the temperature, precipitation and wind bearing of that city to calculate a color representation of it.

Once the color is made, Space Nietzsche tweets it up with its favorite hashtag "#colorweather". Every so offten, Space Nietzsche also randomly selects another hashtag and it even makes a friend from its followers!!
