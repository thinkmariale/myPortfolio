Research

Smoke

When I was around 13 years old I saw an amazing NVIDIA commercial which involved intense smoke rendering. After seeing that commercial I became passionate about smoke. At the end of my third year in school I decided to fulfill my passion by starting to research participating media so that I could create my own smoke renderings with the use of photon mapping (Reseach Page).


FusionSkyGames

FusionSkyGames was a result of a research I did in human computer interaction (HCI) to increase learning in children in developing countries and economically challenged areas. After five months of HCI research and field study in Bogotá, Colombia and Troy, NY; I applied my learning's and conclusions in a game called NumberMission. During the development of the game I continued doing field study with the children of Bogotá, Colombia and Troy to guarantee that my game would impact kids and increase learning in both math and recycling (the main focus of the game). (WebPage).
