Computer Graphics

Computer Graphics is one of my favorite fields of study. I love that it is a never-ending challenge, in which the harder the problem the more beautiful the reward. It is in this field that makes my inner artist comes to life! Participating Media is one of the fields in Computer Graphics that blows my mind. I love staring at smoke and at the clouds and just imagine how hard they must be to render. I took this as a challenge and did an independent research in smoke ring renderings and it was a great learning experience!!


  • Independent StudyRPI

    Smoke Rendering

    An Octree representation of a smoke ring rendering made as an independent research project.

    When I was around 13 years old I saw an amazing NVIDIA commercial which involved intense smoke rendering. After seeing that commercial I became passionate about smoke. At the end of my third year in school I decided to fulfill my passion by starting to research participating media so that I could create my own smoke renderings with the use of photon mapping.
